COLLEGE TRANSPORTATION

Sri Eshwar College of Engineering, Coimbatore, provides comfortable, reliable and safe transportation for students, staff and faculty. Buses are available from various locations including Bharathiar University, Dhali, Edyarpalayam, Jallipatti, Kalapatti, Kovaipudur, Kudimangalam, Madathukulam, Palladam, Periyanaikanpalayam, Pollachi, Ponneri, Udumalpet, Saravanampatti, Sivananda Colony, Sulur, Tiruppur, Vadavalli, Vallakundapuram, Veerapandi and Vellalore.
